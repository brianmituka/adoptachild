About Us

adopt a child is a non-profit organization founded in 2014 by Brian Mituka and Carole Akoth to help save children from the streets by rehabilitating them and then finding them parents. The parent selection process is a rigorous one and we do it with the help of various government and international agencies to help us with background checks on the prospective parents to ensure that the children are not going to be mistreated and that they are in good hands.
